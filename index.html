About

Developer, builder, observer.

I'm Brandon, a developer who likes working close to practical systems: AI infrastructure, robotics, LLM inference, optimization, and tools that make engineering work clearer.

In open source, I'm interested in the kind of work that helps people ship and understand things: readable docs, reproducible examples, focused issues, useful pull requests, and notes that turn scattered learning into something another developer can use.

Away from the terminal, I make room for photography, birding, science fiction, music, vegetarian cooking, and time outdoors. That mix keeps me curious and gives me more ways to notice patterns, details, and systems.
